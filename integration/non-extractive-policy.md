# Non-Extractive Integration Policy

Any attempt to:
- Aggregate logic
- Centralize execution
- Enforce synchronization
- Introduce orchestration

is considered a violation
of MAXIMAL-ONE principles.

---

## Allowed Actions

- Declaration
- Recognition
- Reference
- Silence

---

## Forbidden Actions

- Control
- Optimization
- Scaling pressure
- Monetization triggers

---

Presence precedes usefulness.
Always.
